One project history for the team

By the end of commissioning, copies of the program usually live scattered on laptops and USB drives.

Spyke gives the team one shared place for every saved version, with who added it, when, and a short note on why. Anyone on the project can open it and see where things stand.

Review before handover

Before the customer gets the program, the team can walk through the differences together. Teammates comment on individual changes, question anything that looks off, and agree on the version being delivered.

Engineers who were never on site can still take part in the review.

RD Swapped the reset interlock on R12 — can you check it against the FAT spec?
KV Matches. One nit — add a note on the new debounce timer.

Support after handover

When a customer calls a year later, the first question is whether the program still matches what you left behind. Export the program currently running at the site and compare it with the as-left version.

Spyke shows whether the logic has changed since handover — and if it has, where to begin investigating.

ConvRun := StartPB AND NOT EStop;
- InfeedDelay := T#200ms;
+ InfeedDelay := T#450ms;
RejectPulse := T#80ms;
Ready := ConvRun AND Homed;
